_Image Description: A spiral galaxy seen close up and almost face
on. It is filled with puffy, patchy clouds of hot gas and dust. Red,
orange and yellow colors indicate light emitted by different
particles. The brightest colors are in the center and along the two
spiral arms, which wind out from the center. Star clusters hide in the
gas along the arms. A few large, bright white stars are prominent in
the foreground, near to us._
