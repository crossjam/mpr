Title: Serial Project Hoarding
Author: C. Ross Jam
Date: 2022-12-08
Status: published

Wisdom from Simon Willison. [Coping strategies for the serial project hoarder][1]

> This is the most important tip: avoid side projects with user accounts.

> If you build something that people can sign into, that’s not a
> side-project, it’s an unpaid job. It’s a very big responsibility,
> avoid at all costs!

> Almost all of my projects right now are open source things that
> people can run on their own machines, because that’s about as far
> away from user accounts as I can get.

> I still have a responsibility for shipping security updates and
> things like that, but at least I’m not holding onto other people’s
> data for them. 

Let me repeat for emphasis **"_If you build something that people can
sign into, that’s not a side-project, it’s an unpaid job._"**

Most definitely.
