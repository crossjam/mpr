Kafka implements partitioned, replicated, append only logs. If you squint enough, those logs can look like a messaging system. Turns out Kafka is pretty good for a lot of distributed system and “big data” use cases.

I couldn’t make it to the inaugural [Kafka Summit][2], but the organizers have made [video recordings and slides available][3] for all of the presentations. Well done!
